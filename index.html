Aii Lanuage Center

First Aii Lanuage Center was open in 2005 and it was Mao Tse Tong Campus. It started with only 20 students in 2005 and by 2021, Aii has over 7,000 students and 1,400 staff, studying and working in 6 campuses all over Phnom Penh and 50,000 alumni. Mengly Jandy Quach is the founder of Aii Lanuage Canter.
